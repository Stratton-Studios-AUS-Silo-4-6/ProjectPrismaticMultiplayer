Spicy Sushi Games
Stylized Stones Assetpack
contains: 
- Stone01 to Stone15
- One Geyser with 3 different Textures (Fire, Toxin, Standard)
- 2 Stone Groups with which you can make cliffs
- Prefabs of every Asset (have 1024x1024 Material, additional 2048x2048 Material is in each Material Folder)
- Ground Textures for >>Terrain Tool<<

To Change to 2K Material: 
1. Open the prefab
2. Drag 2K Material on the Mesh
3. Close the Prefab
